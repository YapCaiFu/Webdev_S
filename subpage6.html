Careers

SENIPA career opportunities


SENIPA is constantly searching for tunnelling and mining specialists, both with a contracting and a design background for projects all around the world.

SENIPA is interested in professionals with expertise in the following fields of work. If you at the same time have the ability to travel abroad for shorter or longer periods then consider which areas of expertise below would fit and send us your CV for consideration.

  • Project management
  • Supervision
  • NATM personnel
  • TBM personnel
  • Mechanical personnel
  • Electrical personnel
  • Designers, consultants
  • various

We are looking for motivated people with the drive to contribute to the company, eager to reach higher levels in SENIPA. SENIPA offers competitive salary and benefits.
If you think you have the qualifications and the interest to work for SENIPA, we invite you to send your resume to the Human Resource Manager, info@senipa.com.
