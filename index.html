About Us


SENIPA's specialty: tunnelling services with guaranteed tunnel advance rates.


  • SENIPA is a dynamic team, focusing its human resources on the tunnelling and mining industry

  • SENIPA is contractor in the tunnelling industry and has in addition - through the vast personnel resources - the capability to execute any kind of tunnel design works.

  • SENIPA supplies tunnelling and mining material and equipment.

  • SENIPA is headed by a team of highly experienced tunnelling engineers from Western Europe, who held management positions in well know construction and engineering firms.

  • SENIPA's general staff consists of Western European experts, complemented by experts from various countries from all continents.

  • SENIPA's experts have experience in the tunnelling and mining industry in all continents.

SENIPA’s Financial Services​
